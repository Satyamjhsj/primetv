Smart TV Setup Guide

Step-by-step instructions to set up your new smart TV

  1. Unbox your TV and attach the stand
  2. Connect to power and turn on the TV
  3. Follow on-screen instructions to connect to WiFi
  4. Sign in to your streaming accounts

Streaming Device Activation

How to set up Roku, Fire TV, Apple TV, and Chromecast

  1. Connect device to your TV's HDMI port
  2. Power on the device and switch to correct input
  3. Follow activation instructions on screen
  4. Visit activation website and enter code
  5. Complete account setup and start streaming
